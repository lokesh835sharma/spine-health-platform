ML Architecture

Model Architecture Overview

01

Data Collection

85 original survey responses from participants on lifestyle, posture, sleep, physical activity, and medical history. Extended to 250 via synthetic data augmentation.

02

Preprocessing Pipeline

Age & Sitting Hours: Median imputation + StandardScaler
Gender: OneHotEncoder (drop first) + StandardScaler
10 categorical features: OrdinalEncoder (ordered categories) + StandardScaler

03

PCA Transformation

PCA reduces 12 scaled features into 2 principal components. PC1 captures pain-related variance (pain, stiffness, family history). PC2 captures lifestyle variance (sitting, OTT, activity).

04

K-Means Clustering (k=4)

K-Means++ initialization with random_state=42. Optimal k=4 selected via Elbow Method. Evaluated using Silhouette Score, Davies-Bouldin Index, and Calinski-Harabasz Index.

05

Cluster Labeling

Clusters labeled by analyzing mean feature values per cluster:
Cluster 0 → High Risk · Cluster 1 → Very High Risk · Cluster 2 → Low Risk · Cluster 3 → Moderate Risk

Other Algorithms Explored
🌊

DBSCAN

Density-based clustering — effective for irregular shapes but sensitive to epsilon parameter on this dataset.

🌳

Hierarchical Clustering

Agglomerative clustering with Ward linkage. Dendrogram confirmed 4-cluster structure, validating K-Means choice.

🎲

Gaussian Mixture Models

Probabilistic clustering that assigns soft cluster memberships. Provided similar 4-cluster structure with probability scores.
